Lina Filipovich is a multidisciplinary artist and musician, born in Minsk, Belarus, and based in Paris. Her practice spans electronic music, textile, installation, screen printing, and found footage film. Her work explores the deconstruction of religious iconography through a feminist and post-religious lens. Drawing from icons and biblical narratives, Lina seeks to liberate these symbols from their patriarchal context and reframe them within a feminist mythology. Using textile techniques and recycled fabrics, she fragments sacred codes and reconfigures them into new mythological forms.
