Lina Filipovich is a multidisciplinary artist and musician, born in Minsk, Belarus, and based in Paris. Her practice spans electronic music, textile, installation, screen printing, and found footage film. Her work explores the deconstruction of religious iconography through a feminist and post-religious lens.
